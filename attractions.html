Ropes Course

This tower of fun is three stories tall and features a ropes bridge along with other challenging elements.

Requirements

Minimum 48" tall. Guests 44" tall may participate with an adult 18 years of age and at least 48" tall. Both participants require a ticket. Proper footwear is required. Guests cannot participate in socks or bare feet. No sandals, flip-flops, high heels, crocs, open toed shoes, etc.
