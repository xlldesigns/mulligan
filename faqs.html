Park FAQS & Policies

Park FAQs & Policies

Birthday Parties

What's the minimum number of guests?
You must pay for a minimum of ten guests to participate in the package. There is no extra charge for additional seating for non participating guests; however we will need to know prior to your event so that we may provide you with enough room. Seating for non-participating guests cannot be guaranteed, but we will do our best to accommodate your group.
Can I order additional food off the menu?
Absolutely. We offer a number of platters and specials that can be found on our Special Event Menu.
Do we eat or play first?
Either way is fine. We recommend table time is scheduled for the beginning of your party. Your host/hostess is responsible for taking care of the party only during your table time. At the end of the table time you will receive your wristbands where your party will be free to go and enjoy the rest of the park.
Can I bring in outside food?
We do not allow outside food. Cake and ice cream are the only exception as long as a party package has been purchased.
Can I take my leftover food and cake?
We are more than happy to box any leftovers and store them in our refrigerator until you leave the park.
Do you supply a cake or can I bring my own?
Cake is not included in our packages, but you can supply your own as long as you have a birthday package.
Is there a deposit required?
Yes. We do require a $40.00, non-refundable deposit. Please bring your deposit within 72 hours of making your reservation. We do not accept personal checks.
Do you provide invitations?
We will provide you with free invitations that are in postcard form with our address and map so that you may deliver those to your guests.
Are the party tables decorated?
Yes. Mulligan will decorate your party tables with plates, cups, napkins, table tent name plate and utensils. You are more than welcome to bring in your own party supplies. Please let us know in advance. Bringing your own party decorations will not decrease your party charge. Balloons can be added to your table for $6.99 per bouquet. One bouquet includes 1 Mylar balloon and 4 Regular ‘Mulligan’ Balloons.

General

Do you have any weekly specials?
Every Tuesday we have $12 Tuesday. See $12 Tuesday flyer.
Do you have a rain policy?
In the event that management makes the decision to close an attraction due to inclement weather, guests may exchange their outdoor attraction tickets for one of the following items:
You may save your Fun Card and use it on another day.
If you have purchased an unlimited use wristband you may exchange it for a rain check that is good for another day.
Can I bring in outside food or drink?
No outside food or drink is permitted on the premises.
What method of payment do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express and Cash. We do not accept personal checks.
