“FUN” Raising

Is your organization, club, church, or group looking for a fun way to raise money? Why not let us organize all the details for an entertaining fundraiser?

Sell tickets for a profit

Available on Friday, Saturday, or Sunday. Choose a 4 hour time period to host your event.

4 Hour ticket includes UNLIMITED Go-Karts, Rock Wall, Miniature Golf, Rookie Go-Karts, Lazer Tag, Ropes Course, and Blaster Boats*.

A Mulligan specialist will create a flyer and print tickets for your event.

Your cost of the ticket is $15.99 (regularly priced at $29.99).

You choose the selling price of your ticket to determine your profit.

Minimum of 50 tickets must be sold.

Reservations can be made over the phone at (661) 273-1407. Valid Tax ID required. Contact Crystina Dumas for more information (661) 273-1407 ext. 201 or email at c.dumas@mulliganfun.com

*Blaster Boats are a seasonal attraction and may not be available at all times. ** Only video arcade games are included. Does NOT include prize-redemption games – games that dispense tickets or prizes.

Grad Night

Have a graduation night party for your school at Mulligan with a variety of continuous entertainment, activities, prizes, and food. It is our goal to reduce tragedies by providing a fun, safe, and sober environment to celebrate their achievement.

Check out our standard package below, but remember we can customize the Grad Night Party Package to fit your school’s needs!

12:00 AM – 4:00 AM
Includes Unlimited:

Go-Karts
Lazer Tag
Miniature Golf
Rock Wall
Ropes Course
Video Arcade*
*excludes prize redemption games.

Up to 200 participants

$24.99 per each additional participant
