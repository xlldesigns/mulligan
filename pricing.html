Pricing

Fun Cards are a great way to save money!

Receive bonus money when you spend at least $25. The more you spend, the higher your bonus!

Use your Fun Card for all attractions, arcade games, and food and beverage. Plus, your tickets are automatically added to your card, so you can go directly to the redemption counter to get your prizes! You can also register your card at the kiosk so we can replace it if you lose it.


Fun Card Packages

There is a $0.50 activation fee for NEW Mulligan Fun Cards.
*Bonus value can be used for arcade & individual attraction tickets.


Individual Tickets

Each ticket is good for one attraction


Go-Karts

$7.50

Drivers must be at least 56″ tall for the single karts. Drivers for the double karts must be 56″ and 18 years old. Passenger on 2-seater karts must be a minimum of 40″ tall, 4 years of age and have good upper body control.


Double Kart Passenger

$3.50

Must be 40″ or taller


Rookie Track

$7.50

Must be 40″, maximum 54″


Lazer Tag

$7.50 per session


Blaster Boats

$7.50 - Driver (minimum 44”)

$3.50 - Passenger (maximum 44”)

Maximum weight 275 lbs. Passenger must be a minimum of 40″/maximum of 44″ and 45 lbs and 3 years of age. Driver with passenger must be a 18 years of age. The Blaster Boats are seasonal.


Miniature Golf

$10.00 - Adult (Ages 10 & Over)

$7.00 - Child (Ages 9 & Under)


Rock Wall

$7.50 for 3 attempts.

Must be 75lbs – 300lbs. Guests must wear appropriate shoes for climbing at all times. Guests cannot participate in socks or bare feet. No sandals, flip-flops, high heels, crocs, open toed shoes, etc.


Batting Cages

6 year old minimum

$1.00 per session (12 pitches)

Hourly Team Rates

$25.00 per cage for 1 Hour (with minimum 48 Hour Reservation)

$35.00 per cage for 1 Hour (less than 48 Hour Reservation, based on availability)


Ropes Course

$10.00 per session

Minimum 48″ tall. Guests 44″ tall may participate with an adult 18 years of age and at least 48″ tall. Both participants require a ticket. Proper footwear is required. Guests cannot participate in socks or bare feet. No sandals, flip-flops, high heels, crocs, open toed shoes, etc.


All Day All Access Pass

Unlimited Lazer Tag, Go-Karts, Rookie Go-Karts, Blaster Boats (Seasonal Attraction), Ropes Course, Rock Wall and Miniature Golf

Only $29.99 Per Person - Available ALL DAY Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays during normal business hours. All Day passes are available every day during Summer and select school break periods.
